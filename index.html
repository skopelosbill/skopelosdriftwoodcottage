Introducing Driftwood Cottage

Click on any image to enter the slideshow of higher resolution images.

Driftwood Cottage was built over 100 years ago and it has retained its Greek charm. The location in the heart of town is wonderful, within minutes of walking to everything. There are no stairs from the port to the cottage, making it family friendly. Although so centrally located , it is in a car free street, and it is in a very quiet area. It sleeps 5 people in 3 air-contitioned bedrooms with 2 bathrooms and wifi. It also offers a roof terrace, with sea views, which has a fully equipped kitchen including dishwaser and wasing machine and a barbecue with an area to relax and also dine in.
